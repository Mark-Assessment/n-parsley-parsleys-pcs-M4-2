Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is Parsley's PCs?

Parsley's PCs is an online store specializing in custom-built computers and computer components. We offer a wide range of products from top brands to meet your computing needs.

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team by emailing support@parsleys-pcs.com or by calling us at (123) 456-7890. Our support team is available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM.

Orders and Shipping

How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply add the desired products to your cart and proceed to checkout. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your purchase.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Stripe.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary depending on your location and the shipping method selected at checkout. Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days, while expedited shipping options are available for faster delivery.

Returns and Refunds

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most products. To initiate a return, please contact our customer support team with your order details and reason for the return. Please note that certain items may be subject to a restocking fee.

How do I get a refund?

Once your return is received and inspected, we will process your refund. The refund will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase. Please allow 5-7 business days for the refund to appear in your account.

Account and Privacy

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" link at the top of the page and follow the instructions to register. An account allows you to track orders, save shipping addresses, and receive updates on new products and promotions.

How is my personal information used?

Your personal information is used to process orders, provide customer support, and improve our services. We do not share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal obligations. For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
